# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, education, socio-economic status, or identity.

## Standards
- Demonstrate empathy and kindness.
- Be respectful of differing opinions.
- Focus on what is best for the community.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to **conduct@debuggix.com**.
